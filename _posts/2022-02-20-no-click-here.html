Your links should always contain descriptive text that lets the user know what information they will find after clicking the link.

Not everyone is clicking

Links are styled differently so they stand out. People scan the page, but scanning 7 “click here”s doesn’t help them know what your content is.

When you tab through a page, it skips from link to link and reads the link text to users. This is useless if the link text isn’t descriptive.
