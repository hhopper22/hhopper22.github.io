Background

In early 2022 the university president announced his No. 1 Priority: Continued improvement of student experience and success.

"President Sherman is committed to expanding opportunities for every student to build a portfolio of experiences during their time at OHIO, closing equity gaps in student success, and ensuring students graduate on time and ready to move into graduate studies or their career of choice."

Goals

Design, develop, and flesh out, and launch an all-new Experience OHIO website that lives directly off of ohio.edu.

  • design, development, content sourcing and creation, and content input in a 3-month timeline
  • design and develop two new web page layouts - one interior page that displays data for each individual experience and one landing page with search and filtering capabilities
  • launch with a minimum of 35 experiences
  • create a new Drupal content type with 17 fields, four new taxonomies with 52 associated terms, and three new view blocks
