This website project aims to showcase unique learning opportunities available to Ohio University students. I worked to design and develop two new web page layouts - one interior page that displays data for each individual experience and one landing page with search and filtering capabilities - and a 'featured experience' layout to display on the university website homepage.

Adaptable Details

The icons and colors in this "Details" section of an individual Experience page change depending on the information input into Drupal for the specific experience. These features give site users quick details through visual cues.

Project Background

In early 2022 the university president announced his No. 1 Priority: Continued improvement of student experience and success.

"President Sherman is committed to expanding opportunities for every student to build a portfolio of experiences during their time at OHIO, closing equity gaps in student success, and ensuring students graduate on time and ready to move into graduate studies or their career of choice."

In order to deliver on our brand promise and support President Shermanâ€™s No. 1 priority, we leveraged the university website to showcase the myriad of unique learning opportunities available to Ohio University students.

Goals

Design, develop, flesh out, and launch an all-new Experience OHIO website that lives directly off of ohio.edu.

  • design, development, content sourcing and creation, and content input in a 3-month timeline
  • design and develop two new web page layouts - one interior page that displays data for each individual experience and one landing page with search and filtering capabilities
  • launch with a minimum of 35 experiences
  • create a new Drupal content type with 17 fields, four new taxonomies with 52 associated terms, and three new view blocks

Results

  • The site received more than 5500 pageviews within the first month
  • Average time on page in the first month was 75 seconds
  • More than 50 experiences were included in the site at launch
  • This site is now used in serving our prospective student audience for recruitment, yield, and melt efforts
  • The site is being integrated into many high-profile locations on the ohio.edu website, such as the Academics landing page and various college navigations
  • I was able to incorporate an entirely different facet of our official brand into our homepage through the inclusion of topographic shapes
