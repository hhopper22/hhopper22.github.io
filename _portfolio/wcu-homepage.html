Project Goals

Goals included in this redesign and development overhaul project:

  • Surface content marketing and news content
  • Surface important links for different audience groups
  • Reduce content repetition
  • Refresh the look and feel of the page to modernize the style and provide more breathing room while staying true to the university's brand
  • Add a promotional area for important non-story content
  • Provide a sense of familiarity in the updated design
  • Reduce load time of the page
  • Improve event representation and information
