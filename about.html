I am a web and graphic designer and front-end developer. I have over nine years of experience in various areas of design including web, digital, and print design, and have eight years of service in the higher education landscape.

I enjoy the complex artistry of web design and the structure and exactness of front-end code. I crave organization and I'm motivated by helping others achieve their goals.

These days, I spend the majority of my working time supporting the style, structure, functionality, and technical advancement of an enterprise-wide university website with 200+ CMS editors, as well as designing and building new pages and sites for high-level initiatives. I am open to taking on additional projects, especially if they allow me to explore new topics! Contact me if you'd like to discuss your project.

I like good paper, home DIY projects, and strawberry cheesecake. (And I don't think that fruit should be on pizza.)
